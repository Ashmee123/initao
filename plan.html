Plan Your Visit to Initao

Getting Here

Initao is easily accessible by land transportation from major cities in Northern Mindanao. The nearest major airport is Laguindingan Airport (CGY), approximately an hour's drive away. Buses and vans regularly travel to Initao from Cagayan de Oro City and other neighboring towns.

Transportation Options:

  • Bus: Regular bus services from Cagayan de Oro City.
  • Van: Faster van services are also available.
  • Taxi/Car Rental: Available from Laguindingan Airport.

Accommodation

Initao offers a range of accommodation options to suit different budgets and preferences. It's recommended to book in advance, especially during peak season.

Where to Stay:

  • Beach Resorts: For a relaxing stay by the sea.
  • Guesthouses: Budget-friendly options in the town center.
  • Hotels: Comfortable amenities and convenient locations.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Initao is during the dry season, which typically runs from November to May, offering sunny skies and calm seas ideal for beach activities and exploration. The shoulder months of June and October can also be pleasant, with fewer crowds.

Local Tips

To make the most of your visit, consider these local tips:

  • Try the fresh seafood available at local restaurants.
  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Bring appropriate attire for the beach and cave explorations.
  • Stay hydrated, especially during outdoor activities.
  • Learn a few basic Filipino phrases for a more enriching experience.
  • Bargaining is common in local markets.
