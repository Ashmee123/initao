Welcome to Initao Municipality!

Initao is a beautiful municipality located in the province of Misamis Oriental, Philippines. It is renowned for its stunning natural landscapes, ranging from pristine beaches with crystal-clear waters to lush forests and intriguing caves. One of its most famous attractions is the Initao-Libertad Protected Landscape and Seascape, a vast area protected for its unique biodiversity.

Here, you can engage in various activities such as spelunking (cave exploration), snorkeling in the clear waters, and trekking through the forest trails. The municipality also boasts several resorts and recreational areas perfect for families and friends looking for relaxation or adventure.

Beyond its natural beauty, Initao is also home to warm and friendly locals. Don't miss the chance to savor fresh seafood at local eateries and explore the bustling markets to experience the authentic culture of Initao. A visit to Initao is guaranteed to be an unforgettable journey.
